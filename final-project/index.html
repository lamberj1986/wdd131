The Great Harvet Bread Co. in Grand Rapids is being brought back to the area. Our family grew up enjoying the fresh baked bread and we missed it when moving to Grand Rapids in 2020. We are working on opening up a new franchise of this great store so that our great city can enjoy the daily fresh baked bread again.

Our Great Harvest will not look like any other Great Harvest out there. We will operate under a "Freedom Franchise." This "Freedom" part means that we get to operate our store in a manner that will meet the unique needs of this community. We will develop unique recipies that will only be found at our location, in addition to this we plan on offering other local fares at this location. That way you can get the feel of going to a farmer's market year round.

Owned and operated by a 20+ year Air Force Vetran and his family, we know what it takes to serve the community and we are dedicated to finding new ways to give back. We look forward to seeing you in store once we open.
