Site Name

Great Harvest Bread Co - Grand Rapids, Michigan
This name has been chosen to help differentiate between the other Great Harvest Bread franchise locations around the US.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help begin the marketing material for the new Grand Rapids franchise location of Great Harvest Bread Co. Once the store is open, a variety of fresh baked goods will be offered ranging from loaves of bread to fresh sandwiches and cookies.

What is Great Harvest Bread Co?
Great Harvest Bread does things differently, from the fresh milled flour to the daily baked bread, we feel that the quality of the ingredients make a better sandwich. Give us a try and you'll soon understand what we mean.
